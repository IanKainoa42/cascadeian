Quick help

How do I move and fire? Tap a trooper to select it, then tap a glowing green hex to move (or drag a path). Tap outside the green to point that trooper's line of sight down a lane. Troopers spot and fire on enemies automatically once they're in view.

What are the colored hexes? Green = where the selected trooper can move. Red = dangerous ground under enemy fire. Dark = fog of war you can't see into.

Real-time vs turn-based: Choose your mode in the match setup screen. Real-time plays in relaxed continuous time; turn-based alternates turns.

My progress / settings: Everything is saved on your device automatically.
