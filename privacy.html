Privacy Policy — Hex Squad Lanes

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Hex Squad Lanes is a single-player hex-grid tactics game. Your privacy matters, and the short version is simple: the app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

Information we collect

None. Hex Squad Lanes has no user accounts, no sign-in, and no registration. We do not collect names, email addresses, contacts, location, device identifiers, usage analytics, or any other personal data.

Data stored on your device

The game saves your settings and preferences (such as gameplay options and tuning) locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. Deleting the app removes it.

Third parties, ads, and tracking

Hex Squad Lanes contains no advertising, no third-party analytics, and no tracking of any kind. We do not use the App Tracking Transparency framework because we do not track you.

Children

The app is safe for all ages and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If a future version of the app adds features that involve data (for example, online multiplayer), this policy will be updated before those features ship, and this page will describe exactly what is involved.
